[REVIEW | dorm=1019 Commonwealth Avenue | date=2020-01-01 | room_type=double]
1019 is a great place to live as a sophomore. You typically get 3 doubles to a suite, with a living room and shared bathroom. The doubles are a decent size (for sure bigger than Warren/West type of thing), and you get a lot of counter space in the bathroom. I would say mostly sophs and juniors live here, and it's a smaller building (only 6 floors) so you never really have to compete to do laundry or wait for the elevator for too long. There's lots of fun take out/food spots in the area (rip to ur wallet), but it is super far west, and a lot of people don't enjoy the extra 10min walking (5ish min to the bus at stuvi2). Also, you have to go to Rich to get your mail, which isn't bad since you have to go there for the dining hall anyway.
