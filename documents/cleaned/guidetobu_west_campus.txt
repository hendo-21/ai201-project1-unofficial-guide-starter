## West Campus > Overview of West Campus at BU
West Campus is a vibrant residential area that's home to many Boston University (BU) students. Located along Commonwealth Avenue, this part of campus is served by the Green Line's B branch, making it easy to get around. West Campus is known for its iconic dorms like Warren Towers, Claflin, and Sleeper Hall, as well as a variety of dining options and student hangout spots. Whether you're a freshman looking to immerse yourself in the BU experience or an upperclassman seeking a lively community, West Campus has a lot to offer.

## West Campus > Dorms on West Campus
The most well-known dorm on West Campus is undoubtedly Warren Towers, a massive complex that houses over 2,000 freshmen. Warren is a BU landmark, with its distinctive high-rise design and convenient location. Other popular West Campus dorms include:
Claflin Hall: A traditional-style residence hall popular with sophomores and juniors.
Sleeper Hall: A high-rise dorm with suite-style living, often home to upperclassmen.
Shelton Hall: A modern, apartment-style dorm with private bathrooms.
While the dorms on West Campus vary in style and amenities, they all provide a vibrant, on-campus living experience close to classes, dining, and activities.

## West Campus > Dining on West Campus
West Campus is home to some of BU's most popular dining options. The crown jewel is Marciano Commons, a massive dining hall that serves up a wide variety of cuisines in an open, modern setting. Other notable West Campus eateries include:
Warren Towers Dining: A classic college dining hall located right in the Warren Towers complex.
Rhett's: A lively on-campus pub and restaurant known for its burgers and craft beers.
FitRec Café: A healthy dining option located in the nearby Fitness & Recreation Center.
Whether you're craving comfort food, healthy options, or a fun social scene, the dining spots on West Campus have you covered.

## West Campus > Amenities and Hangout Spots
Beyond the dorms and dining halls, West Campus offers plenty of other amenities and hangout spots for students:
BU Beach: A grassy area and popular gathering spot located just off Comm Ave.
George Sherman Union (GSU): The student center, home to shops, study spaces, and event venues.
Fitness & Recreation Center (FitRec): A state-of-the-art gym and sports complex.
West Campus Green: A scenic park-like area perfect for relaxing or playing frisbee.
With its vibrant atmosphere and convenient access to campus resources, West Campus provides everything students need to make the most of their BU experience.

Living on West Campus means being at the heart of the BU student experience. The area is bustling with activity, from sports games and concerts to student organization meetings and social events. As a West Campus resident, you'll be immersed in the energy of campus life:
