## Danielsen Hall > Welcome to Danielsen Hall: BU's Historic Residence
Built in 1939, Danielsen Hall is a classic example of Georgian Revival architecture, with its distinctive brick façade and columned entryway along the vibrant Comm Ave corridor. Named after Norwegian-American philanthropist Olav Danielsen, who donated funds for its construction, the dorm now houses over 300 undergraduate students each year at Boston University (BU).
As one of the university's oldest and most iconic residences, Danielsen offers a unique blend of historic character and modern amenities. Its prime location on BU campus and tight-knit community make it a highly sought-after housing option for students looking to live in a space with real personality and connection to the university's past.

## Danielsen Hall > Ideal Location on Comm Ave
One of Danielsen Hall's biggest draws is its unbeatable location right on the Comm Ave corridor. As part of BU's central campus, Danielsen is just steps away from key academic buildings, dining halls, and student hubs like the George Sherman Union (GSU).
Students living in Danielsen have easy access to the Green Line's B branch, with the BU Central and BU West T stops right outside the dorm. This makes it a breeze to get around campus and explore the rest of Boston. The dorm is also conveniently located near major shopping and dining destinations along Comm Ave, like the Target and Chipotle.
With its prime spot in the heart of campus, Danielsen Hall provides residents with the ultimate in convenience and community. You'll be right in the middle of the BU action, without having to travel far for your daily needs.

Twin XL beds
Desks and chairs
Dressers and closets

Students also have access to several communal lounges, study areas, and laundry facilities throughout the dorm. The building's grand lobby features cozy seating and a fireplace, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere.
One of the standout amenities in Danielsen is the spacious, well-equipped kitchen on the first floor. Residents can prepare home-cooked meals here, providing a nice break from the dining hall fare. The kitchen is a popular gathering spot for students to socialize and bond over shared meals.
While Danielsen maintains its historic charm, the dorm offers the modern conveniences and comforts that today's students expect. It's a harmonious blend of old and new that helps make residential life here so appealing.

## Danielsen Hall > A Tight-Knit, Spirited Community
What really sets Danielsen Hall apart is the strong sense of community that permeates the dorm. As one of BU's smaller, more intimate residences, Danielsen fosters a close-knit environment where everyone tends to know each other.
'The community here is unparalleled,' says Sarah, a current Danielsen resident. 'Everyone is so friendly and welcoming. It really feels like a home away from home.'

Danielsen's community spirit also manifests in the dorm's many beloved traditions. ## Danielsen Hall > Things to Consider About Danielsen Hall
While Danielsen Hall offers an unparalleled on-campus living experience, there are a few key factors to weigh when deciding if this dorm is the right fit for you:
Size: As one of BU's smaller residences, Danielsen can feel quite cozy - which some students love, but others may find claustrophobic. The intimate community vibe isn't for everyone.
Age: Danielsen's vintage architecture and historic character are part of its charm, but the dorm's age also means the rooms and amenities may not be as modern as newer BU housing options.
Location: Danielsen's prime Comm Ave location is incredibly convenient, but the constant foot traffic and street noise can be disruptive for light sleepers.
Dining: While Danielsen has a fully equipped kitchen, there are no on-site dining halls. Residents will need to walk to Marciano Commons or other campus eateries for meals.
Overall, Danielsen Hall provides a uniquely immersive BU experience with its historic setting, tight-knit community, and unbeatable location. But the dorm's intimate size and vintage character won't appeal to every student's preferences. Carefully weigh the pros and cons to see if Danielsen is the right housing choice for you.
