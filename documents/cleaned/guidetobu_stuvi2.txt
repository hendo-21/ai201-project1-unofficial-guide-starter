## Stuvi2 > Navigating the Stuvi2 Housing Lottery
Pro Tips
With Stuvi2 being one of the most desirable dorms on campus, getting a spot can be a competitive and stressful process. The housing lottery system at BU determines who gets to live in Stuvi2, and spots go quickly.
Here are a few tips for increasing your chances in the Stuvi2 housing lottery:
Apply early: The earlier you submit your housing application, the better your chances of securing a Stuvi2 room.
Aim for an upperclassman draw time: Upperclassmen generally have priority in the housing lottery, so try to get as high a draw number as possible.
Consider a double or triple room: Single rooms in Stuvi2 are the most competitive, so you may have better luck with a double or triple.
Be flexible: If you don't get your first choice, keep an open mind. The other BU dorms are great options too.
Have a backup plan: Don't put all your eggs in the Stuvi2 basket. Research other on-campus housing options in case you don't get selected.
