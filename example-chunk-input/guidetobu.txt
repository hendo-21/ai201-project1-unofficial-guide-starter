## Stuvi2 > Introducing Stuvi2: BU's Iconic High-Rise Dorms
If you're a Boston University (BU) student, chances are you've heard of Stuvi2 - one of the most iconic and recognizable dorms on campus. Officially known as the Student Village II, these twin high-rise towers are home to over 1,100 undergraduate students and have become a staple of the BU experience.
Located right in the heart of campus on Comm Ave, Stuvi2 offers a prime location just steps away from classes, dining halls, and all the action. As one of BU's largest and most sought-after dorms, getting a spot in Stuvi2 is a badge of honor for many students. With its modern amenities, vibrant community, and unbeatable convenience, it's easy to see why Stuvi2 is a favorite among Terriers.
