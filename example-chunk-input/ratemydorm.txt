[REVIEW | dorm=Bay State Road Brownstones | date=2025-01-01 | room_type=double]
Arguably the best location on campus, close to almost every major classroom building/lecture hall. Never walked more than 10 minutes to class except to CFA/CGS. The room is quite spacious and the building is quiet. However, it feels quite shabby, desperately needing renovation. There is only one 3-prong outlet for the entire room. There is laundry in the basement, but aside from that, it is really lacking in amenities. The bathroom is decent, obviously offering more privacy than a large dorm bathroom, but it's a bit outdated. When picking a brownstone, keep in mind that they are all quite different, so be careful to compare floor plans and see if the one you are choosing has been renovated yet - it makes a big difference.
